What is Web Development?

Web development consists of front-end and back-end

Front end gives you the capability of presenting information in an infinite number of ways

Back end helps you manipulate the information before presenting it: it may be simple compute, or a large language model generating the answer to the prompt

What will you get out of Web Development?

The ability to quickly whip up a functioning website looks good on your resume

You start to understand web design and pay attention to designers' choices on the websites

You will think like a software engineer! (Well, it depends on how deeply you delve into computer science concepts)
